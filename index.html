Helping Families Help Themselves...
Since 1897

Santa Maria Community Services provides Greater Price Hill with educational tools and resources to build strong families, promote healthy residents and foster neighborhood revitalization. For more than 114 years Santa Maria has helped families help themselves.

To read about Santa Maria in the news, click here!

Santa Maria

  • Provides support to more than 4,000 individuals annually;
  • Operates from four office locations in Price Hill and Sedamsville/Riverside area;
  • Employs 40 staff and engages more than 500 volunteers who give more than 20,000 hours of their time annually.

 

In 2007 Santa Maria was chosen to be a lead agency in Early Childhood/Youth Development area in Place Matters initiative
